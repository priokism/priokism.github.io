Forum dan Komunitas Pertanian Indonesia – Agroteknologiwebid LezoLimo waqala ngokuzimeleyo kwiindawo ezahlukeneyo zomhlaba, yaye ziquka uluhlu olwahlukeneyo zohlaza. Ubuncinane 11 kwiingingqi ezahlukeneyo eNdala Yehlabathi Elitsha ababebandakanyeka njengamaziko elizimeleyo lokuzalwa. iinkozo Wild zaqokelelwa kwaye ukuqwengwa ubuncinane iminyaka 105.000 eyadlulayo. Iihagu zazifuyiwe eMesopotamiya ngeenxa 15,000 eyadlulayo. Rice zazifuyiwe eTshayina phakathi 13.500 kwaye 8.200 kwiminyaka eyadlulayo, ilandelwe Bu, iimbotyi neembotyi azuki. Izimvu zazifuyiwe eMesopotamiya phakathi 13.000 yeminyaka kwaye 11,000 eyadlulayo. Ukusuka kwi-11,500 eyadlulayo, izityalo ezisibhozo umnyibilikisi PERIOD, wawenziwe kunye einkorn ingqolowa, nerhasi Wawumde, iinuts, ilentils, grass ekrakra, ertyisi tsheri neflakisi kulinywe kwi Levant. Imfuyo zasekhaya ukusuka engasekhoyo zasendle kwiinkalo Turkey zanamhlanje Pakistan kwiminyaka ethile 10.500 eyadlulayo. KwiiAndes eMzantsi Merika, iitapile zazifuyiwe phakathi 10,000 yaye-7,000 eyadlulayo, kunye neembotyi, coca, yeellamas, iialpacas, kunye neehagu Guinea. Izele leswekile kunye imifuno ingcambu zaye esikweni New Guinea ngeenxa 9,000 eminyaka eyadlulayo. Amazimba zazifuyiwe kummandla Sahel eAfrika engama-7,000 eyadlulayo. Umqhaphu zazifuyiwe ePeru ngeminyaka 5.600 eyadlulayo, yaye ngokuzimeleyo zazifuyiwe Eurasia ngexesha engaziwayo. EMesoamerica, teosinte zasendle zazifuyiwe umbona ngeminyaka 6000 eyadlulayo.
Impucuko yaba imveliso ye Agricultural PERIOD Revolution; njengoko H. G. Wells esitsho, impucuko yaba surplus yezolimo. Ekuqhubeni imbali, ngempucuko ungqamene emajukujukwini kunye neendawo echumayo njenge Uchumileyo Crescent, kwaye ithi kwasekwa ikakhulu kumazwe circumscribed yezolimo. The Great Wall of China kunye limes ubukhosi laseRoma xa (imida) esikelwe kumda efanayo kumantla ipapa ezolimo.
